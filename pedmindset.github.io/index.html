    MY CAREER STORY

    Before i went to the university i worked for two companies. in these companies i learned most of what i know today. Whiles at the University i decided to start a company to build website for companies and make a bit of cash to support myself.

    That how i started PED Solutions LTD

    PED Solutions LTD was my First Company, the experience with PED has made who i am today and given me the values i used to approach business problems.

    During my time in PED i discovered the importance of creating value for clients and developing strong relationships with them that goes beyond the boundaries of the office setting, of course i had no office so i literally had to go beyond the office setup :) ...

    My PED Experience has thought me about some important things when it comes to solving problems.

    - Solution must affect revenue directly or indirectly.
    - Solution must increase productivity and decrease waste and Cost.
    - Solution must affect the public image( with respect to Client Customers, Competitions, Employees, Society, Good will etc) of the client positively.
    - Solution must exceed client expectation.
